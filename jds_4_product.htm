Standard Bicycles

Standard bikes are the most common types of bicycles used today. A standard bike has two wheels that are mounted in-line in a metal frame, a saddle seat, and is propelled by pedals connected to the rear wheel by a chain. Chade's offers a variety of standard bikes, including road bikes, racing bikes, mountain bikes, and cruisers.

Prices starting at $300

Specialty Bicycles

Specialty bikes are cycles that fill a specific need and are for customers looking for something more non-traditional. We understand that “one size never fits all” and offer a range of special niche items, whether it be tandem bikes, cargo bikes, quadra-cycles, and special needs bikes.
