Chade's Bicycle Company FAQ

How long will it take to build my custom bike?

We aim to have your bike ready in 3-4 weeks after your design completion.

Is there a multi-bike discount if I wanted to buy more than one bike?

Yes, we can provide a discount! Contact our shop and let us know what type of bikes you are interested in purchasing and we can let you know what discount we can offer.

Is there a warranty on your bicycles?

There is a one-year warranty for the components and a limited lifetime warranty for the bicycle frame.

Do you sell children’s bikes?

No, we currently do not offer children’s bikes for sale.

Is a deposit required when I rent a bicycle?

Yes, we require a $50 deposit to rent one of our bikes, as well as a valid ID. The renter is responsible for any damage caused to the bike while in their possession.

Do I need to wear a helmet when renting a bicycle?

Yes, helmets are provided with all rentals and riders are required to wear them.
